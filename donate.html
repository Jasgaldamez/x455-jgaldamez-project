Your Impact

At Chico Animal Shelter, your donations are a lifeline for our furry residents. Your support directly impacts their lives by:

Medical Care

By contributing to our medical care fund, you directly impact the lives of shelter animals, helping us provide essential treatments, surgeries, vaccinations, and preventive care. Your support makes a tangible difference in their health and happiness, giving them a chance at a brighter future.

Food and Shelter

Your generous contributions make a world of difference by ensuring that our furry residents have access to nutritious meals and a safe haven. With your support, we can provide essential sustenance and comfortable shelter, offering our animals the care they need while they await their forever homes.

Enrichment Toys

By contributing to our toy and enrichment fund, you directly enhance their mental and emotional well-being, providing them with stimulating activities and playful moments. Your support helps create happier and healthier animals while they await their forever homes.
